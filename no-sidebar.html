Picture Editing Using GIMP

My Experience

One of the projects I worked on involved using GIMP to edit a photo of myself into a laboratory scene. Learning tools like GIMP, which is open-source and freely available, has been both practical and enjoyable. Features such as the lasso tool, adding alpha channels, and layering techniques are not only essential for effective editing but also a lot of fun to experiment with.
